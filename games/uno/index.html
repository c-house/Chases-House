How to play

Match a color or a number.

Each turn, play one card from your hand that matches the discard pile's color or its number/symbol. If you can't play, draw a card. First player to empty their hand wins the round.

Action cards: Skip blocks the next player. Reverse flips direction. +2 forces the next player to draw two cards (and skip). Wild lets you choose any color. Wild +4 forces the next player to draw four and choose a color.

Don't forget UNO! When you play your second-to-last card, hit the UNO button before any opponent catches you. Get caught and you draw two.
