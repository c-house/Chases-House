How to play

Roll five dice up to three times each turn, holding any you want to keep between rolls. After your rolls, score the dice in one of the 13 categories on your scorecard. Each category can be used once.

Upper Section

For Aces through Sixes, score the sum of dice matching that face. Score 63 or more in the upper section for a +35 bonus.

Lower Section

  • 3 of a Kind — sum of all dice (need ≥3 matching).
  • 4 of a Kind — sum of all dice (need ≥4 matching).
  • Full House — 25 points (3 of one face + 2 of another).
  • Small Straight — 30 points (4 consecutive faces).
  • Large Straight — 40 points (5 consecutive faces).
  • Yahtzee — 50 points (5 of a kind).
  • Chance — sum of all dice, no requirement.

Yahtzee Bonus

If you score 50 in your Yahtzee box and later roll another five-of-a-kind, you earn an additional +100 points. Subsequent Yahtzees follow the joker rule: must be scored in the matching upper box if open; otherwise any open lower category at face value.

Controls

  • Space roll · 1–5 hold dice · ↑/↓ select category · Enter commit · Esc pause
  • Mouse / touch: tap a die to hold; tap a scorecard row to score.
  • Gamepad: A roll/confirm · D-pad to navigate dice + scorecard.
