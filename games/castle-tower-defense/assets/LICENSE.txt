  Filter criteria: docs/adr/028-castle-tower-defense-3d.md §11. The
  Pizzicato + Sax jingles (acoustic-led, modal major) satisfy "Stardew-warm";
  Bell impacts give the cannon/castle their weight without being harsh.

  BGM (bgm_loop) and ambient (ambient_loop) deferred to a follow-up
  — audio.js degrades gracefully when missing.
