How to Play

Defend thy keep against ten waves of marching foes. Build towers along the road, hold the line, and conserve thy lives — three stars await those who lose none.

Towers
  • Archer Fast single-target. Tier 3 looses two arrows at once.
  • Cannon Slow splash damage. Strikes ground only — flying foes pass unharmed.
  • Mage Magic damage that ignores armour and chains between enemies at higher tiers.
  • Frost Slows enemies. Tier 3 may freeze on hit.
Foes
  • GoblinBaseline. Anything kills it.
  • OrcHeavy and slow — favour Cannon splash or focused fire.
  • Wolf-riderFast and fragile. Frost slow keeps them in your range.
  • ShieldedArmour resists physical damage. Mages punch through.
  • FlyingBypasses ground towers. Archers and Mages can hit them.
  • WarlordMap 3 boss. Regenerates unless steadily damaged.
Controls
Click a slot then a tower card — or press 1–4 — to build.
Click a placed tower to upgrade or sell. Keys: U upgrade · S sell.
Space pause · N send next wave (+5 gold) · F fast-forward · Esc menu.
Xbox controller supported: D-pad / stick to move cursor, A confirm, B cancel, X/Y upgrade/sell, START pause.
