How to Play

Three heroes. Five turns. Three villagers to protect. Every enemy attack is shown one turn before it lands — push, pull, and swap to make those attacks miss, hit walls, or hit each other. You may undo any action before pressing Confirm Turn.

Knight pushes the unit on an adjacent tile away by one. Archer pulls a unit on a clear line toward herself by one. Mage swaps two adjacent occupied tiles within range 3. Each hero may move and act in the same turn, in either order.
